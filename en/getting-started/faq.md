# Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions that may come up during registration and first login.

---

## Registration

**I didn't receive an invitation email**

Check your Spam or Junk folder. If it's not there — contact your account manager or open a ticket in [Support](https://console.cloud-native.kz/support).

---

**The invitation link doesn't work or has expired**

Invitation links are single-use and expire after a limited period. Contact your account manager or support to request a new invitation.

---

**The email pre-filled in the registration form is wrong**

Do not continue with registration. Contact whoever sent the invitation — they will need to send a new one to the correct address.

---

**The system won't accept my password**

Your password must be at least 8 characters long. Make sure the password and confirmation match.

---

## Logging In

**I forgot my password**

Email [cns-support@fcd.kz](mailto:cns-support@fcd.kz) — the support team will help you regain access.

---

**After logging in I get redirected back to the login page**

Try clearing your browser cache and cookies. If the issue persists — open a ticket in [Support](https://console.cloud-native.kz/support).

---

**I see an error after login even though my credentials are correct**

Make sure you are using an up-to-date browser. Try opening the console in incognito mode. If that doesn't help — contact support.

---

## Organization & Access

**I see the wrong organization**

There is a dropdown menu in the top-right corner of the console for switching between organizations. Make sure the correct one is selected.

---

**I want to change my account email**

Contact [Support](https://console.cloud-native.kz/support) — email changes are handled manually.

---

**I need to invite colleagues but don't see that option**

Inviting members requires the **Owner** or **Administrator** role. If you have the Member role — ask an Administrator in your organization.

---

## Billing

**I can't find where to create a Billing Account**

Go to **Billing → Payment Accounts** and click **Create Account**. Full instructions are in [First Steps](./first-steps.md).

---

**Why do I need to choose Individual or Legal Entity?**

The account type affects your document flow: legal entities receive invoices and acts of work. Legal entities also have access to extended platform terms and capabilities.

---

**I want to pay by bank transfer but that option isn't available**

This feature is currently in development and will be available soon. For now, bank card payment is available.

---

> Didn't find your answer? Open a ticket in the [Support](https://console.cloud-native.kz/support) section.

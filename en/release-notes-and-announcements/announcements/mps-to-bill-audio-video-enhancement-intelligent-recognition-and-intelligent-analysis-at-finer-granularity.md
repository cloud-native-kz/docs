**Billing Explanation**

- Billing Rule: Charges are based on the resolution and duration of the video after enhancement, with a daily billing cycle.
- Billing Formula: Video Enhancement Processing Fee = Output File Duration (minutes) × Output File Different Resolution Unit Price (USD/minute).
- For each video enhancement task executed, only one fee is charged. Within a billing cycle, if the duration is less than one minute, it will be calculated as one minute.
